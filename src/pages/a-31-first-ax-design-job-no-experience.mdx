## The Short Answer

Get an AX design job without AX experience by building an AI-focused portfolio project, positioning yourself as a UX designer with AI skills, and applying to companies that are hiring for AI design roles. The title doesn't matter — your portfolio and skills do.

---

## Step 1: Build One AI Project

You don't need a full portfolio overhaul. Build one AI-focused project:

- An AI chatbot interface with conversation flows
- A prompt design system for a specific domain
- An AI-powered research tool prototype
- An agent interaction design with error states and trust signals

This one project proves you understand AI design. It's enough to get interviews.

---

## Step 2: Update Your Brand

Update your headline, LinkedIn, and portfolio to say "UX Designer | AI Product Design" instead of just "UX Designer." This signals to recruiters that you're AX-ready.

---

## Step 3: Apply to the Right Jobs

Look for these job titles:
- AI Product Designer
- AI UX Designer
- Agent Experience Designer (rare but growing)
- Conversational Designer
- Interaction Designer (AI)
- UX Designer (AI)

Use the [job board](/jobs) to find these roles.

---

## Step 4: Network in AI Design Communities

Join AX design communities on LinkedIn, Twitter, and Slack. Participate in discussions about AI design. This gets you noticed by hiring managers.

---

## Step 5: Be Honest About Your Experience

You don't have AX job experience. That's fine. Frame it as: "I'm a UX designer who has been actively building AI design skills and projects." This is honest and shows initiative.

---

## FAQ

### How long does it take to land your first AX job?
3–6 months with consistent effort: portfolio building, applications, and networking.

### Do I need to learn coding to get an AX job?
No. Understanding AI concepts is more important than coding ability.

### Can I get an AX job without a design degree?
Yes. AX design values practical AI design skills over formal education.

### What's the most important thing to show in my portfolio?
One strong AI project that demonstrates you understand AI product design principles.
