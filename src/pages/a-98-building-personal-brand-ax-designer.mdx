## The Short Answer

Build your personal brand around one thing: AI-era UX design. Create one high-quality piece of content per week that showcases your expertise. Start with LinkedIn and build consistently.

## Positioning

### Your Niche
- Agent UX design

- Conversational UX

### Your Differentiator
- Real projects with AI

- Consistent output

## Content Strategy

### Weekly Content
- One deep-dive article (your flagship content)
- Two LinkedIn posts (key insights)
- One Twitter thread (trending topics)

### Monthly Content
- One case study (project showcase)
- One resource list (curated tools)
- One community post (engagement)

## The Bottom Line

Your personal brand as an AX designer is your most valuable career asset. Build it consistently, focus on one niche, and let your work speak for itself.
