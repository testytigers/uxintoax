## The Short Answer

Agency UX gives you breadth — you work on many projects across different industries. In-house UX gives you depth — you go deep on one product and its strategy. In the AI era, agency work is accelerating because AI tools let agencies deliver higher quality faster. But in-house roles offer more strategic impact and higher compensation at the senior level.

---

## Agency UX: Breadth Over Depth

### What You Gain

- **Variety** — Different industries, different clients, different problems
- **Speed** — AI tools let agencies deliver more projects in less time
- **Exposure** — See how different companies solve similar problems
- **Network** — Work with multiple client teams and stakeholders
- **Specialization options** — Focus on specific tools, industries, or problem types

### What You Lose

- **Depth** — Shallow engagement with each product
- **Ownership** — Clients own the product; you design for them
- **Long-term impact** — Harder to see the outcomes of your designs
- **Team cohesion** — You're often embedded in client teams, not your own

---

## In-House UX: Depth Over Breadth

### What You Gain

- **Ownership** — Your designs ship and affect real users
- **Depth** — Deep understanding of one product's users and metrics
- **Strategic impact** — You influence product direction, not just screens
- **Team cohesion** — You work with the same engineers and product managers
- **Growth visibility** — You see how your work affects metrics over time

### What You Lose

- **Variety** — You work on one product or product family
- **Exposure** — Limited to your company's industry and approach
- **Speed** — Larger products mean longer design cycles
- **Client skills** — Less client-facing work means fewer consulting skills

---

## The AI Factor

AI tools change the calculus for both paths.

**For agencies:** AI lets one designer deliver what used to require two. This means agencies can take on more projects, charge better rates, and scale without headcount growth. Agency designers who use AI effectively become more valuable.

**For in-house:** AI lets one designer handle more of the research, content, and prototyping that used to require specialists. In-house designers who use AI effectively can operate with more autonomy and impact.

---

## The Bottom Line

Start in agency for breadth, move in-house for depth. This is the career path that gives you both perspectives. AI accelerates both models, so the timing of your switch matters less than the experience you bring to each role.
