## The Short Answer

AI saves 30-50% of your UX work time when used strategically. Here's how to use it for each part of your workflow.

---

## Research: 90% Faster

### Before AI
- Transcribe interviews: 2 hours per interview
- Code transcripts: 4 hours per study
- Generate reports: 8 hours per study

### With AI
- Transcribe: 10 minutes (Otter.ai)
- Code: 5 minutes (Claude analysis)
- Report: 2 minutes (structured output)

**Time saved: 13 hours per study**

---

## Design: 50% Faster

### Before AI
- Wireframing: 4 hours per screen
- Content generation: 2 hours per screen
- Prototyping: 6 hours per flow

### With AI
- Wireframing: 1 hour (Figma AI)
- Content: 10 minutes (ChatGPT)
- Prototyping: 3 hours (AI-augmented tools)

**Time saved: 9 hours per screen**

---

## Testing: 60% Faster

### Before AI
- Test script creation: 3 hours
- Analysis: 5 hours
- Reporting: 4 hours

### With AI
- Script: 15 minutes (ChatGPT)
- Analysis: 10 minutes (Claude)
- Reporting: 5 minutes (structured output)

**Time saved: 11 hours per study**

---

## Communication: 40% Faster

### Before AI
- Design rationale: 2 hours
- Stakeholder updates: 1 hour
- Documentation: 3 hours

### With AI
- Rationale: 15 minutes (ChatGPT)
- Updates: 10 minutes (ChatGPT)
- Documentation: 30 minutes (AI-assisted)

**Time saved: 4 hours per project**

## The Bottom Line

AI saves 30-50% of your UX work time. The biggest gains are in research analysis and content generation. Start by using AI for your biggest time sinks — the tasks that take the most hours per project.
