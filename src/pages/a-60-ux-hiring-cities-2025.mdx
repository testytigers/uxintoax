## The Short Answer

The top UX hiring cities are San Francisco (18%), New York (12%), and Seattle (10%). But the fastest-growing cities are Austin (+35%), Denver (+25%), and Miami (+20%). The best value cities combine solid salaries with low cost of living: Austin, Denver, Miami, and Raleigh. Remote work is making city choice less relevant, but the smartest approach is to target remote roles or high-value cities.
