## The Short Answer

AI tools for UX design are getting better and more accessible every month. Here are 12 free tools that can improve your workflow — from research analysis to prototyping to content generation. All have genuinely free tiers that individual designers can use without a credit card.

---

## Research Tools

### 1. ChatGPT (Free)

Free tier includes GPT-4o access with usage limits. Excellent for research analysis, survey synthesis, interview theme extraction, and competitive analysis. The most versatile AI tool for UX designers.

**Best for:** Research analysis, content generation, brainstorming

### 2. Claude (Free)

Free tier offers 200K token context window — perfect for processing large research documents. Better than ChatGPT for structured output and following complex instructions.

**Best for:** Research synthesis, structured analysis, instruction following

### 3. Otter.ai (Free)

Free transcription tool for interviews and focus groups. Records and transcribes conversations in real-time. Free tier includes 300 minutes per month.

**Best for:** Interview transcription, meeting notes, focus group recording

---

## Design and Prototyping Tools

### 4. Galileo AI (Free Tier)

Generates UI designs from text descriptions. Free tier includes 100 credits per month. Integrates with Figma. Great for rapid exploration and concept validation.

**Best for:** Rapid UI exploration, concept generation, Figma integration

### 5. Uizard (Free Tier)

AI-powered design tool that generates UI from sketches, text descriptions, or templates. Free tier includes 2 projects. Great for non-designers and rapid prototyping.

**Best for:** Sketch-to-design, rapid prototyping, non-designer collaboration

### 6. Visily (Free)

AI design tool that converts screenshots into editable designs. Free tier available. Great for competitor analysis and inspiration.

**Best for:** Screenshot-to-design, competitor analysis, design inspiration

---

## Content and Copy Tools

### 7. Jasper (Free Trial)

AI copywriting tool for interface copy, help text, and error messages. Free trial includes 2,000 words. Excellent for generating interface content.

**Best for:** Interface copy, error messages, help text generation

### 8. Grammarly (Free)

AI grammar checker for UX writing. Free tier includes basic grammar and spelling checks. Essential for ensuring interface copy is clear and correct.

**Best for:** UX writing quality, grammar checking, clarity improvements

---

## Testing and Analytics Tools

### 9. Maze (Free Trial)

Usability testing platform with AI analysis features. Free trial includes 1 test. Great for conducting and analyzing usability tests.

**Best for:** Usability testing, task analysis, feedback collection

### 10. Hotjar (Free Tier)

Behavioral analytics with AI-powered insights. Free tier includes 1,000 pageviews per day. Heatmaps, session recordings, and funnel analysis.

**Best for:** Behavioral analytics, heatmaps, session recordings

---

## Bonus Tools

### 11. Midjourney (Free Trial)

AI image generation for mood boards, concept art, and visual exploration. Free trial includes limited generations. Not a design tool, but great for creative exploration.

**Best for:** Mood boards, concept art, visual exploration

### 12. Canva (Free)

AI-powered design tool for presentations, documentation, and visual assets. Free tier is comprehensive. Great for creating presentations, research summaries, and team documentation.

**Best for:** Presentations, documentation, visual assets, team communication

---

## The Bottom Line

You don't need expensive tools to do great UX design with AI. These 12 free tools cover the entire UX workflow — research, design, content, and testing. Start with the ones that solve your biggest pain points and expand from there.
