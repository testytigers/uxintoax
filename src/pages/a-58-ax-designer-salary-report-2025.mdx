## The Short Answer

The global median salary for UX designers in 2025 is $120K USD. AI UX roles pay 20-40% more than traditional roles at the same level. Junior AI UX designers earn $90K in the US. AI UX Design Leads earn $215K — a 43% premium over traditional leads.
