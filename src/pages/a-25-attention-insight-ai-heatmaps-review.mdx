## What is Attention Insight?

Attention Insight is an AI-powered tool that predicts where users will look on a webpage before it's built. It uses computer vision and eye-tracking data from thousands of real users to generate heatmap predictions for any design. You upload your design, and within seconds you get a heatmap showing predicted attention zones, fixation points, and visual hierarchy.

---

## What Designers Actually Use It For

### Visual Hierarchy Validation

**What it does:** Upload your design and see where users are predicted to look first, second, and third.

**What we found:** This is the most useful application. We used Attention Insight to validate visual hierarchy on 3 projects. The predictions weren't perfect, but they were good enough to catch major issues — like a CTA button that the AI predicted would be overlooked because it wasn't positioned in a high-attention zone.

**Time saved:** ~1 hour per design validation

### A/B Testing Predictions

**What it does:** Compare two design variants and predict which one will capture more attention.

**What we found:** Surprisingly accurate for simple comparisons. When we compared two landing page variants, the AI correctly predicted which one would perform better in 2 out of 3 cases. The predictions are more useful for major design changes than for subtle tweaks.

**Time saved:** ~2 hours per A/B test prediction

### Design System Audits

**What it does:** Run the same design through Attention Insight multiple times to identify patterns in how different components capture attention.

**What we found:** Useful for design system audits. We uploaded 10+ design system components and analyzed which ones captured the most attention. The data helped us optimize our component library for visual impact.

**Time saved:** ~3 hours per audit

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- **Unique tool** — no direct competitor offers this exact feature
- **Fast predictions** — results in seconds, not hours
- **Good for major design changes** — predictions are more accurate for significant visual changes
- **Figma integration** — works within your existing workflow
- **Data-driven design decisions** — adds objective data to subjective design opinions

### Cons

- **Not a replacement for usability testing** — predictions are estimates, not actual user behavior
- **Less accurate for minor changes** — predictions are less reliable for small visual tweaks
- **Limited customization** — can't adjust the AI model or provide custom training data
- **No demographic targeting** — predictions are based on aggregate data, not specific audience segments

## Alternatives Worth Considering

### Eye-tracking tools (Tobii, etc.)

For actual eye-tracking studies, dedicated tools like Tobii are more accurate but significantly more expensive and complex.

### Hotjar

For real heatmaps from actual users, Hotjar provides real usage data. It's the opposite of Attention Insight — real vs predicted.

## FAQ

### How accurate are Attention Insight's predictions?

The predictions are roughly 80% accurate for major design changes and 60% accurate for minor changes. They're useful for catching obvious issues, not for making fine-grained decisions.

### Can I use Attention Insight for mobile designs?

Yes. Attention Insight supports both desktop and mobile designs.

### Does Attention Insight work with Figma?

Yes. You can upload Figma designs directly without exporting.

### Is Attention Insight safe for client work?

Yes. Upload designs securely and get predictions without sharing your designs publicly.
