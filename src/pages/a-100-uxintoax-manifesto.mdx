## The uxintoax Manifesto

### We Believe Design Is Changing

AI is reshaping how products are designed and experienced. The interfaces of tomorrow look different from the interfaces of today. They're conversational, adaptive, and intelligent. And the designers who create them are called AX designers — Agent Experience designers.

### We Believe Every Designer Can Adapt

The transition from UX to AX isn't about learning to code. It's about expanding your thinking. It's about understanding that AI isn't replacing designers — it's creating a new discipline that designers have always needed.

### We Believe Knowledge Should Be Free

The best design education shouldn't be behind paywalls. We believe every designer deserves access to high-quality, practical knowledge about AI-era design — regardless of their background, budget, or location.

### We Believe in Practical Knowledge

We don't write theory. We write what works. Every article is based on real experience, real projects, and real results. If it doesn't help designers do better work, it doesn't belong here.

### We Believe in Community

The AX transition is a collective journey. No single designer, company, or course can teach everything. We're building a community where designers share knowledge, challenge each other, and grow together.

### We Believe in Consistency

One article per week. One email per week. No fluff. We respect your time by delivering focused, actionable content that you can actually use.

### We Believe the Future Is Now

AI isn't coming for design. It's already here. The designers who are adapting now will lead the field in the next decade. The designers who wait will fall behind.

### We're uxintoax

We're a platform for UX designers who are ready for the AI era. We build content, tools, and community to help you thrive.

**This is the new discipline. This is the new platform. This is the new era.**
