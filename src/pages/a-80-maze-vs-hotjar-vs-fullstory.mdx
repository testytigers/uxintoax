## The Short Answer

Maze is the usability testing specialist. Hotjar is the behavioral analytics generalist. FullStory is the enterprise session replay platform. Each serves a different purpose, but Maze is the best standalone tool for usability testing, Hotjar is the best for quick behavioral insights, and FullStory is best for enterprise teams that need deep session analysis.

---

## Maze: Usability Testing Specialist

### What Maze Does Best
- Usability testing at scale (moderated and unmoderated)
- Task tracking and success measurement
- Feedback collection with sentiment analysis
- Prototype testing (Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD)
- Benchmarking against industry standards

### Best For
- UX researchers who need structured usability tests
- Teams that test multiple prototypes
- Remote usability testing at scale

---

## Hotjar: Behavioral Analytics

### What Hotjar Does Best
- Heatmaps for clicks, scrolls, and movement
- Session recordings of real user behavior
- Funnel analysis for conversion optimization
- Quick, low-friction insights
- Small-to-mid size teams

### Best For
- Product teams who want quick behavioral insights
- Marketing and growth teams
- Teams with limited research resources

---

## FullStory: Enterprise Session Replay

### What FullStory Does Best
- Enterprise-scale session replay
- Advanced analytics and segmentation
- Technical issue detection (errors, crashes)
- Integration with enterprise tools
- Compliance and data governance

### Best For
- Large enterprises with complex products
- Teams that need deep session analysis
- Organizations with compliance requirements

## The Comparison

| Feature | Maze | Hotjar | FullStory |
|---|---|---|---|
| Usability testing | Excellent | Good | Limited |
| Session recording | Basic | Excellent | Excellent |
| Heatmaps | No | Excellent | Good |
| Enterprise scale | Good | Medium | Excellent |
| Ease of use | Easy | Easy | Complex |
| Price | Moderate | Affordable | Expensive |

---

## The Bottom Line

Pick based on your primary need: Maze for usability testing, Hotjar for quick behavioral insights, FullStory for enterprise analysis. Most teams start with Hotjar or Maze and add the other as needs grow. AI tools are making all three platforms smarter — Maze uses AI for test analysis, Hotjar for insight automation, FullStory for pattern detection.
