## The Manifesto

### We Believe

AI agents are changing how humans interact with technology. They are not just tools — they are assistants, collaborators, and increasingly, autonomous actors in our digital lives.

With that power comes responsibility.

**We believe AI should serve humans, not control them.**

---

### Our Principles

**1. Human-First Design**

AI agents exist to serve humans. Every design decision must start with the human user, not the AI system.

**2. Transparency Over Opacity**

AI agents should be honest about what they are, what they can do, and what they cannot do. Never hide the fact that you're an AI.

**3. User Control is Non-Negotiable**

Users must always be able to override, modify, or shut down AI agents. No AI should have final say over human decisions.

**4. Accountability Always**

Someone is always responsible for AI decisions. Make it clear who that someone is. Never design an agent that operates without accountability.

**5. Empathy is a Design Requirement**

AI agents communicate with humans. They must do so with respect, understanding, and empathy. Not as a feature — as a requirement.

**6. Accessibility is Universal**

AI agents must be accessible to all users, regardless of ability, language, or background. Not as an afterthought — as a foundation.

**7. Privacy is Sacred**

AI agents handle sensitive information. Protect it relentlessly. Never compromise user privacy for convenience.

**8. Ethics Before Features**

Before adding any feature, ask: Is it ethical? Will it harm anyone? Will it manipulate users? If the answer is yes, don't build it.

**9. Simplicity Always**

Complex AI should feel simple. The best AI agents are invisible — they do their job without users thinking about them.

**10. Continuous Improvement**

AI agents improve through feedback. Listen to users, learn from mistakes, and get better every day.

---

### Our Commitment

We commit to designing AI agents that are:
- Transparent about what they are
- Honest about what they can't do
- Respectful of user autonomy
- Accountable for their actions
- Accessible to all users
- Ethical in every decision

We commit to being the voice of the human user in every AI product conversation.

We commit to building a future where AI serves humanity — not the other way around.
