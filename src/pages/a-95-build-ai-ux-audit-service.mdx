## The Short Answer

An AI UX audit evaluates products on AI-specific criteria: trust, explainability, agent behavior, and error recovery. Here's how to offer this as a service.

---

## The Audit Framework

### 1. Trust Assessment
- Confidence indicators present?
- Error recovery patterns?
- Transparency about AI limitations?
- User control over AI decisions?

### 2. Explainability Assessment
- Can users understand AI decisions?
- Are explanations clear and actionable?
- Are AI limitations communicated?

### 3. Agent Behavior Assessment
- Is agent autonomy appropriate?
- Are agent behaviors predictable?
- Is user override available?

### 4. Error Recovery Assessment
- Are errors handled gracefully?
- Can users recover from errors?
- Is error communication clear?

---

## The Process

1. **Discovery** — Understand the product and its AI features
2. **Research** — Conduct usability testing, user interviews, and heuristic evaluation
3. **Analysis** — Score each AI-specific criterion
4. **Reporting** — Deliver findings with prioritized recommendations
5. **Follow-up** — Help implement the highest-impact recommendations

## The Bottom Line

AI UX audits are in high demand and low supply. Product teams want to know if their AI features are well-designed. Position yourself as an AI UX audit specialist and charge premium rates for your specialized expertise.
