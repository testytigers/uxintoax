## The Short Answer

The best AI + UX courses teach agent design, trust patterns, and human-AI interaction — not just how to use AI tools. Here's how to choose the right course for your goals.

---

## What to Look For

### Curriculum
- Agent UX design
- Trust and explainability patterns
- Human-AI interaction design
- Conversational UX
- AI-augmented research and design

### Format
- Self-paced vs. cohort-based
- Hands-on projects
- Mentorship
- Portfolio building

### Outcome
- Career transition support
- Job placement assistance
- Community access
- Certification

---

## Course Categories

### Foundational Courses
Cover UX fundamentals + AI basics. Best for designers who want to understand AI's impact on their field.

### Specialized Courses
Focus on agent design, trust patterns, or conversational UX. Best for designers who want specific AX skills.

### Comprehensive Programs
End-to-end AX design programs covering the full range of AI-era UX skills. Best for career transitions.

---

## The Bottom Line

Choose a course that matches your experience level and career goals. If you're transitioning to AX design, invest in a comprehensive program with portfolio projects. If you want specific skills, choose a specialized course.
