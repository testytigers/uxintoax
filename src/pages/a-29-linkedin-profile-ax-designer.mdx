## The Short Answer

Your LinkedIn needs three changes: (1) a headline that mentions AX or AI design, (2) an about section that positions you as an AX professional, and (3) a featured section with your best AI project. Here are exact templates you can copy.

---

## Headline Templates

**Option 1 (Direct):**
> AX Designer | AI Product Design | Agent Experience

**Option 2 (Hybrid):**
> UX Designer → AX Designer | AI Product Design | Conversation Design

**Option 3 (Senior):**
> Senior AX Designer | Designing AI Products That Users Trust | Agent Experience

**Option 4 (Freelance):**
> AX Design Consultant | Helping Companies Design Better AI Products | Prompt Design | Conversation Design

Choose the one that matches your experience level and career goals.

## FAQ

### Should I change my job title to "AX Designer"?

Only if your role actually involves AX work. If you're still primarily a UX designer, use "UX Designer → AX Designer" or "UX Designer | AI Product Design" instead of claiming a title that doesn't match your day-to-day work.

### How often should I update my LinkedIn?

Update it immediately when you change your headline, about section, or skills. Then post about AI design 2–4 times per month to stay visible.

### Is LinkedIn important for AX design jobs?

Very. Many AX design jobs are filled through LinkedIn referrals and recruiter outreach. Your LinkedIn profile is your #1 discovery tool for AX opportunities.

### What's the best LinkedIn post strategy for AX designers?

Post weekly about AI design. Share case studies, design insights, and career advice. This builds your authority and attracts hiring managers.
