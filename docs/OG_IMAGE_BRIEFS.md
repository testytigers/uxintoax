## OG Image Guidelines

**Colors:**
- Primary: #6C5CE7 (purple)
- Secondary: #00D2D3 (teal)
- Background: #1A1A2E (dark navy)
- Text: #FFFFFF (white)

**Fonts:**
- Headlines: Bold, sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or similar)
- Body: Regular, sans-serif

**Layout:**
1. Dark gradient background (top to bottom)
2. Accent shape in background (circle, abstract)
3. Title text centered, large
4. Subtitle or icon below title
5. Small logo/watermark in corner

**Size:** 1200 × 630px
**Format:** PNG
**File size:** Under 200KB
